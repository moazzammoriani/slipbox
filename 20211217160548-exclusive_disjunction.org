#+title: Exclusive disjunction

The exclusive disjunction of two statements is true only if one of the statements is true. The truth table of the exclusive disjunction is defined as follows.

$P$|$Q$|$P\bigoplus Q$
:-- | -- | --------:
F|F|F
F|T|T
T|F|T
T|T|F
