Overoptimization compulsion is the feeling or need to focus on the irrellevant areas while[[Learning|learning]] in a way that leads your progression to freeze. The freezing usaually happens because the learning task is usually too demanding and the reward not high enough at that point in time to sustain interest and hence the lack of motivation simply leads to freezing.
